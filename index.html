Recipe

Ingredients

  • 55 grams cocoa powder
  • 120 grams hot water
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 grams / 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 95 grams unsalted butter
  • 120 grams warm milk
  • 1 teaspoon Instant coffee powder
  • 175 grams sugar
  • 175 grams AP flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Baking paper / oil for greasing the pan

Instructions

  • Mix coffee powder with hot water
  • Add the mixture to cocoa powder
  • Add the eggs, vanilla extract and softened / melted butter
  • Add warm milk
  • Mix well before seiving the dry ingredients in
  • Add the dry ingredients
  • Mix well
  • Bake in preheated oven at 170°C for 30 mins
  • Remove / stop baking when toothpick comes clean

Extra notes

  • You can use a hand mixer but it isn't required
  • You can add 2-3 tablespoons more flour if batter seems too liquidy
  • You can reduce the amount of hot water too
  • You can follow a different order of wet ingredients too
  • It's okay if it looks a tad lumpy / looks like the butter hasn't dissolved
